Artistic Uncertainty

Artistic Uncertainty is a collaborative project designed to enhance teamwork skills. Our team developed a unique user interface that randomly paints a grid and simulates multiple grids being painted simultaneously. We worked together to record statistics such as the number of grid spaces painted in each color, minimums, maximums, and the percentage of grids filled before running out of paint or other stopping criterion. This project not only showcases my technical abilities but also highlights my experience in effective communication, coordination, and collaboration within a team.
